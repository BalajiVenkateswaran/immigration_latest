I-9 and E-Verify Compliance Solution

Form I-9 and E-verify are the crucial part of any business on boarding procedure. It is the employment Eligibility verification and it is one of the Immigration services forms. Federal law demands employers to verify the employment authorization and identity. One has to show their employers documents from the list of acceptable documents. E-verify is basically an internet-based system which can compare the information from an employees' form I9 to data from the U.S. Department of Homeland Security and Social Security Administration records to authenticate the service.

H1 and H2 Visas

H1B visa is a predominant work visa for professionals and students from in the USA or all over the world. It's a temporary permit for travel or to work in the US in a specialty occupation. US H1B visa remains for an initial period of 3 years and valid up to 6 years. H2B visa is a temporary for short-term or we can say for seasonal work being it as hospitality or tourist industries.

B1 and B2

Business (B1) Visa is a non-immigrant visa to the USA. One who would like to travel to the US for short duration for business associated work like conferences, meetings. But, one should not eligible to conduct meetings or earnings under the B1 visa. B2 is a tourist visa it includes tourism, visit friends or relatives or medical treatment.
